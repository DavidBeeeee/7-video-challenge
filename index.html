Before You Press Record

Five things. That's it.

🔋 My phone is charged and propped up somewhere stable
🪟 I'm facing a window or a decent light source — not a wall
🧹 My background looks intentional — messy is fine if it's your messy
🗣️ I've committed to reading my script out loud before I hit record
